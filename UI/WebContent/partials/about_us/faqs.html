Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Purpleaid?

    Purpleaid wholesale is a E2E software solution for pharma wholesalers. It allows the wholesalers to manage procurement, inventory, sales, collections.

    The easy to use order entry, preview screens, and intutive navigation makes it easy for any staff member to use the system. BI dashboards offer business insights.

    Our role based secutiry and data encryption ensures safety of your data at all times. Purpleaid thus can really boost your revenues.

  • How can Purpleaid help me increase my revenue & profitability?

    1. Inventory & warehouse management - Track and predict product stock needs reducing stock holding to improve cash flow. Currently many distributors are holding 25-35 days (50-100 lacs) stock whereas delivery cycle time from medicine companies is < 5 days. Purpleaid can help reduce stock holding by 40-50% thus freeing up to 20-50 lacs.

    2. Sales Staff empowerment & Tracking - Addressing the pain area at the root by extending order entry to mobile with online / offline (no network connection) access, geo tagging /gps tracking to monitor sales staff movement. Further offers/scheme customization, ranking & payment dues by clients (pharmacists) to improve sales and collection. Purpleaid can boost sales by 20-30%.

    3. Self service - Extends order entry mechanism to end client (pharmacists) so they can also place orders via web, check out offers/schemes, track delivery, payments due and more. Purpleaid can boost sales by 10-20% and can also help in client retention.

    4. BI Dashboards - Bringing in actionable KPIs, trends and patterns of products, geographies, sales staff, collection dues (to improve timely collection & cash inflow) will greatly enable business insight, influence key decisions. This offering out performs any existing solutions by increasing overall revenue & profitability by 40-50%.

    5. Pharma link - Electronically (EDI, Web Service, Email etc) share sales data to medicine companies. Closing the loop helps the whole eco system and mainly helps medicine companies in their sales forecasting/production.

  • Can I install it locally or it has to be on Cloud?

    Purpleaid is designed to work locally as well as on cloud. You have choice to decide which implementation model you would like to follow.

    The key influencing factors for going with CLOUD are Resilience, Availability and Security. We can also help you set up or use existing in house hardware to implement Purpleaid at your premises. We will provide software support and hardware support must be arranged from your existing infra.

    Further, it is possible to migrate from one implementation model to another as per your needs. By exporting/importing your encrypted data elements, we can easily switch between local and CLOUD models.

  • How to transition from old system to Purpleaid?

    If you have existing software in use, you can migrate to Purpleaid using our transition plan.

    1. Implement Purpleaid and Export master data from current solutions and import in Purpleaid. This will save tedious data entry to setup masters.

    2. Start using Purpleaid in pilot mode with limited entries. This is just to ensure that your staff gets trained and knows how to use Purpleaid.

    3. Plan a cutoff date and stop using old system and switch all business transactions to Purpleaid.

    4. If you need, we can set up a small data migration activity to get historical data from old system to new system.

    5. Use Purpleaid and enjoy the ease of use, business enablers and awesome features.
